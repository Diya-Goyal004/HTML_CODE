Hey, I'm Diya
















































I am a student in Software Engineering, Chitkara University.


Here's, what I've done so far.


I made a medical dispenser which can prove to be beneficial for dementia patients. The timer can be modified to suit the patients needs. The alarm rings at that particular time alerting the patient of their medicine. The patient scans there fingerprint to ensure that medicine is being dispensed to the right person. Further, the patient can scan his/ her vitals including body temperature and pulse to ensure that the vitals are checked at the correct time and the medicine is dispensed.



I also built warehouse management robots which can move through the required arena using IR sensors. They can further detect the required shelf with the help of RFID sensors. After the required shelf was recognized, the robotic arm would be used to pick the goods which would be transported back and weighed on the load sensors. Further, RF modules were used to ensure communication between the robots. This project can prove useful for the automation of warehouses and also in handling hazardous tasks.
